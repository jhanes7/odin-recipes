Liquor Ball Sandwiches

World renowned chef Jim Lahey creating the first ever Liquor Ball Sandwich

The recipe combines the great taste of bread and liquor into one

Items needed are:

  • Bread
  • Liquor (Vodka, Highest possible proof)

The steps for creation are:

  1. Cut the crust off the bread
  2. Pour the liquor over the uncrusted bread
  3. Roll into a ball and squeeze excess liquor
  4. Enjoy
